My Story

Hello! My name is Robert Teets, and I am a 25 year old ski bum and software developer. I am currently residing in the beautiful state of Colorado! I've been 'coding' since about 2008, though my projects back then were a lot less... educated. I originally picked up the hobby of coding on a game called Roblox, where I primarily messed around with development in Lua.

Following my early development of my interest in coding, I began trying to expand that knowledge. I spent a good couple years learning to mod games like Garry's Mod using Lua, but quickly realized I was limited by what those games were possible of. This is where I began learning C#, as well as it's uses within Unity.

Most recently, however, I learned of a coding boot camp called Coding Dojo. Currently, I am in the process of learning three different full stacks of technology, ( Python, Java, and MERN ), and I am hoping to fully develop this age-old interest into a career.
